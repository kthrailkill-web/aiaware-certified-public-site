1. Eligibility for Refunds

You may request a refund within 14 days of purchase and only if:

  • You have completed less than 25% of the course, as measured by the LearnWorlds progress‑tracking system (modules marked "complete," videos viewed, quizzes attempted, or materials accessed), and

Refunds are not available after:

  • Completing 25% or more of the course
  • Accessing substantial downloadable materials
  • Receiving a certificate of completion
  • Participating in live sessions, coaching, or cohort-based components (if applicable)
Refunds are not available once a certificate has been issued, regardless of course progress.

2. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are non-refundable:

  • Downloadable templates, checklists, or resources

  • Gift purchases

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email aiaware@aiawarecertified.com with:

  • Your full name

We will review your request and respond within 3–5 business days.

4. Refund Method & Processing Time

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method. Processing times vary by provider but typically take 5–10 business days.

Please note:

  • Some payment processors do not return transaction fees; in such cases, these fees may be deducted from the refund amount.
  • We cannot issue refunds to alternate accounts, cards, or payment methods.
